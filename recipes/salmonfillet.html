Pan seared salmon fillet

Description

Pan Seared Salmon with Garlic Lemon Butter Sauce is one of the tastiest, easiest recipes youâ€™ll ever try! Tender salmon fillets are pan seared until perfectly golden brown then covered in a simple, vibrant and rich lemon butter sauce. A reader favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1x salmon fillet
  • 1x fresh lemon
  • 1x chicken broth
  • 1x garlic
  • 1x block of butter
  • 1x cup of honey
  • 1x olive oil
  • 1x parsley

Steps

  1. Let salmon rest at room temperature
  2. Make lemon butter sauce
  3. Sear salmon fillet. Flip after 2 minutes on each side.
  4. Put sauce on salmon fillet
  5. Enjoy!
